Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GPT.AI

How does GPT.AI handle data security?

GPT.AI employs enterprise-grade encryption for all data in transit and at rest. We follow strict data privacy protocols and are compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and other global privacy regulations. Your data is never used to train our models without explicit consent.

Can I integrate GPT.AI with my existing systems?

Yes, GPT.AI offers robust API access and pre-built integrations with popular platforms like Slack, Microsoft Teams, Salesforce, and more. Our developer documentation provides comprehensive guides for custom integrations.

What languages does GPT.AI support?

GPT.AI supports over 100 languages with near-native fluency, including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Arabic, and many more. Our multilingual capabilities allow for seamless communication across language barriers.

How accurate is the image generation feature?

Our image generation technology produces high-quality, detailed images based on text descriptions. The accuracy depends on the specificity of your prompt, but most users report 90%+ satisfaction with generated images. You can refine results through iterative prompting.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?

Yes, you can change your subscription plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. When upgrading, you'll gain immediate access to new features, and we'll prorate the difference for your current billing period.

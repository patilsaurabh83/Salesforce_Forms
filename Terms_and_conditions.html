Data Privacy

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. By using our services, you agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide during the registration process, including but not limited to your name, email address, contact details, and professional qualifications.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to verify your identity, assess your qualifications, and facilitate the job assignment process. Your data is strictly used for the purpose of our services and is not shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Access to Your Information

After successful registration, you can securely access, correct, or delete your personal information by logging into our app. If you have any concerns or specific requests regarding your data, please don't hesitate to contact our dedicated support team.

Candidature Process

  • Upon registration, your profile will undergo a comprehensive document verification process.
  • Successful completion of assessments and interviews is crucial for the selection process.
  • You'll be selected for a job role based on your performance during these evaluations.
  • The assignment of a job role with your selected company will depend on your demonstrated skills and expertise.
  • We aim to match your qualifications with available positions to ensure a successful and fulfilling role.

Hiring Process Details

  • Registered candidates will receive the candidate details. However, this is not confirmation of candidature since document verification plays a vital role.
  • Only verified candidates will be deployed into the hiring process.
  • Before assessment, you'll receive an email to select the job that you are interested in. If you don't select any opportunities provided, the algorithm will automatically assign you the opportunities as per your experience.
  • Job listing depends on your experience.
  • Assessment selected candidates will get the interview process mail.
  • Candidates who are rejected can't apply for any opportunity for the next 3 months from the rejection.
  • Job listings you'll get are for the respective companies you selected in the registration form.

Important Note

We recommend providing accurate details during the registration process. Incorrect information may lead to disqualification from the process.
